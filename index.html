About the App

MyAnimes is a mobile companion application designed for anime and manga enthusiasts to track their progress, organize planned shows, and rate series with a precise 0.1-step rating scale.

The application features a secure, cloud-enabled Google Drive synchronization system. It stores your watch library and ratings securely within a private, app-specific directory in your own drive, keeping your data safe and private.
