MyAnimes

About the App

MyAnimes is a mobile application designed for anime enthusiasts to track their watched episodes, rate their favorite series and mangas with a precise 0.1-step rating system, and view seasonal stats.

The app features a secure backup and sync utility powered by Google Drive, allowing users to save and restore their libraries automatically in their private application-specific Google Drive directory.
