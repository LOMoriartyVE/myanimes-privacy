Privacy Policy for MyAnimes

Last updated: July 5, 2026

MyAnimes ("we", "our", or "us") provides a watch tracking and rating manager app. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

Google Account Data: When you connect your Google Drive for sync, we access your basic account information (such as your email address) to identify your session.

Google Drive Access: We request access to the drive.appdata (or drive.file) scope. This permission is used exclusively to write and read backup data (your anime lists, history, and preferences) inside a private, application-specific directory on your Google Drive. We cannot see or modify any other files on your Google Drive.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Provide backup and restore synchronization features.
  • Identify your backup files across devices.

3. Data Sharing & Privacy

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties. All backup transactions occur directly between your device and Google Servers using HTTPS.
